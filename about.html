Our Journey

From a Small Workshop
to Surat's Trusted
Furniture Brand

Dev Furniture World began in 2010 with a simple vision: to create furniture that lasts a lifetime. What started as a small workshop in Surat's furniture district has grown into one of the most trusted names in the region.

Our founder, Devbhai Patel, started with a single lathe machine and a passion for woodcraft. Today, we operate a fully equipped manufacturing unit with a team of 50+ skilled craftsmen who share the same passion for quality and precision.

Every piece of furniture we make carries our family's commitment — the right materials, the right process, and the right finish. No shortcuts. Ever.
